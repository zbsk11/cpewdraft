lockdown, eid prayer

eid salah in the park

Last year, as a mosque, we faced the challenge of how to conduct Eid salah (prayer)
while following Covid guidelines. After careful consideration, we decided to hold the prayer outdoors in a nearby park.
This required meticulous planning and coordination with local authorities, but the effort was worth it.
On the day of the prayer, we were overwhelmed with the turnout and the positive feedback we received from the community.

hosted iftar event, impressions hall

muslim belal, talkshow event

This Ramadan, our center hosted a memorable iftar event at Impressions Hall, which sold out completely.
The event was a huge success, with sponsorships from various brands, including Sunah Musk, and raffles held throughout the night.
The main event speaker, Muslim Belal, took to the stage and shared inspiring stories and Nasheeds, captivating the audience.
The night was filled with joy and celebration as members of the community gathered to break their fasts together.
The event was also a fundraiser, and we were thrilled to raise over £30,000 for our center's initiatives.
The overwhelming support from the community, combined with the powerful messages shared by the speakers, made for an unforgettable evening.
Overall, it was a testament to the generosity and spirit of our community, and we look forward to hosting more events like this in the future.

what our donations count towards?

Currently our center facillitates 200 people which we have issues with across prayers. We are raising money to purchase the building next door to expand the mosque. We are at the halfway mark to our £750,000 goal and aim to inshallah buy the bulding this year which will double our total capacity.

At Central Park Community Centre, we strive to provide our community members all the best we can.We are committed to make the community as safe and friendly as possible and try to provide and create a platform for our next generation.
